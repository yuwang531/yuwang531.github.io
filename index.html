Yu Wang

Postdoctoral Associate

Duke University

Biography

I am a postdoctoral associate at Cyber-Physical Systems Lab at Duke University. My research focuses on the analysis and design of high-assurance autonomy using control theory, formal method, statistical methods, and machine learning.

Experience

 
 
 
 
 

Postdoctoral Associate

Electrical and Computer Engineering
Duke University

Advisor: Prof. Miroslav Pajic

  • Developed hyper-temporal-logic-based design/verification algorithms and tools for security and privacy challenges in cyber-physical and robotic systems [TECS/EMSOFT 19], [CSF 21], [ICRA 20], [HSCC 20].
  • Proposed logic-based methods to design attack-resilient supervisors for cyber-physical systems/Internet of things modeled by discrete event systems [CDC 19a], [CDC 19b], [DISS 20].
